INGREDIENTS REQUIRED

1 package dry yeast (about 2 1/4 teaspoons)
1 1/4 cups warm water (100° to 110°)
3 cups bread flour, divided (about 14 1/4 ounces)
1 teaspoon salt
Cooking spray
1 teaspoon cornmeal

DIRECTIONS

Starter:

step 1

Mix water and honey in medium bowl.

step 2

Add yeast; stir to dissolve.

step 3

Let stand until foamy, 10 minutes.

step 4

Add 1/2 cup rye flour and 1/2 cup bread flour; stir to combine.

step 5

Add remaining 1/2 cup rye flour. Mix in enough bread flour to form shaggy mass that can be worked with hands.

step 6

Turn out starter onto floured surface; knead 3 minutes, adding more bread flour if too sticky to work. (Starter will be doughlike in texture at this point.)

step 7

Sprinkle 1 tablespoon bread flour in medium bowl. Add starter dough to bowl. Cover with plastic; let stand at room temperature overnight (starter will lose its shape and spread to a thick batter).
Bread:

step 1

Place 1/4 cup warm water in small bowl. Sprinkle yeast over; stir to dissolve.

step 2

Mix 3 cups bread flour and rye flour in bowl of heavy-duty mixer fitted with dough hook.

step 3

Add yeast mixture and remaining 2 1/2 cups warm water; mix on low speed to blend. Add 1 cup bread flour; beat 4 minutes. Add enough bread flour to form shaggy mass; beat 4 minutes.

step 4

Add salt and starter; continue mixing until dough pulls away from sides of bowl, adding more bread flour if too sticky to form dough, about 5 minutes. Turn out dough onto lightly floured surface; turn to coat. Transfer dough to large bowl. Cover with kitchen towel. Let rise at room temperature 1 hour.

step 5

Generously flour 1 heavy large baking sheet and 1 heavy medium baking sheet. Punch dough down. Turn out onto floured surface. Knead until smooth, 2 minutes.

step 6

Divide into 3 equal portions. Shape each into ball. place 2 balls on large sheet and 1 on medium sheet.

step 7

Cover with kitchen towels. Let rise at room temperature until doubled (test by pressing 2 fingers gently into dough; if indentations remain, dough has risen completely), about 2 hours.

step 8

Preheat ove to 450°F Place baking pan in bottom of oven; add water to create steam. Using sharp knife, cut 3 diagonal slits across top of each loaf.

step 9

Place baking sheet with 2 loaves in oven. Bake until breads are deep brown and sound hollow when tapped on bottom, about 35 minutes.

step 10

Transfer to rack and cool.
